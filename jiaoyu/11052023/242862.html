Meet Alice, a high school senior who has always had a passion for learning. Despite facing various challenges in her life, including financial difficulties and occasional illness, Alice has never wavered in her pursuit of knowledge. She is the type of student who never settles for mediocrity and constantly strives to improve herself.

Alice’s dedication to her education is evident in her daily routine. She wakes up early every morning to review her notes and complete her assignments.
